New 2026-07-21 — a competitor has solved the crossover problem by portfolio. The Zurn brand family now spans both divisions in-house: Zurn, Elkay, Wilkins and Just on the fitting side; Hadrian partitions and World Dryer on the washroom side. The crossover archetype is rare among rep agencies — but a competitor can already walk a specifier through the full package without one. Falsifier not yet run: whether those Division 10 brands share a channel with the fitting lines, or sell separately. If separate, the advantage is nominal.

Source & caveat. Counts derive from a national industry rep directory, twelve competitor rep-locator pages, design-center tenant line cards, and A&D association directories. The directory edition is dated — structural patterns are durable, individual firms require re-verification before any use. Five competitor locators are JS-gated and not yet enumerated, so crossover counts are a floor, not a ceiling.

Enumeration status — 2026-07-21. All five JS-gated locators were re-probed and every one is form-gated: results appear only after a zip or state is submitted. Rendering the page is not enough. The crawlers permitted under our governance can render JavaScript but cannot submit forms, and the tools that can submit forms require account-wide access, which we do not grant. This is a permission boundary, not a tooling failure — it will not resolve on a retry. Every count on this page therefore remains a floor. 100% of the directory-derived roster is past its re-verification threshold and barred from downstream use until individually refreshed.

Governance. Public business information only — agency names, addresses, published line cards. No personal contact data. No account-scoped access to any third-party tool. Snapshot-only; zero writes to T&S corporate systems. Static baseline: this view renders captured values and does not query live.
